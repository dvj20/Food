OVERVIEW

Located on John Street is a cosy eatery that offers a wide variety of Spanish Tapas. You can tell that is all cooked with affection and it feels like you've been magically transported to Spain. In this place, sharing is caring! But warning - it is small, so would suggets booking in advance!

ATMOSPHERE

It is lively place filled with Spanish songs and energetic staff, so it's not just the food that will put a smile on your face. The small size of the restaurant is perfect for a date night or small group of friends, so you can really enjoy spending time with them. The wooden interior and dim lighting make you feel you are on the coast of Spain in a lovely bar...just without the view.
